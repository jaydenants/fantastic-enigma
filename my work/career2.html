Software Developer

Description

A Software Developer is supposed to research, design, develop computer and network systems or to create specialised programs for clients. They are responsible for analyzing client needs and applying that to software development, may also work with computer hardware specialists to integrate software and hardware systems. Another responsibility may be to maintain and update existing software, programs, and databases. May be tasked to work on a project alone or within a larger group or team.

Tasks of job

  1. Design, develop, and modify software systems, using scientific analysis and mathematical models to predict and measure outcomes and consequences of design
  2. Analyze information to determine, recommend, and plan installation of a new system or modification of an existing system
  3. Consult with customers or other departments on project status, proposals, or technical issues, such as software systems design or maintenance
  4. Monitor functioning of equipment to ensure system operates in conformance with specifications.
  5. Supervise and assign work to programmers, designers, technologists, technicians, or other engineering or scientific personnel

Technology skills

  • Object or component oriented development software
  • Web platform development software
  • Database management system software
  • Network monitoring software
  • Process mapping and design software
  • Project management software

Education

Most employers in the field of Software Developers require at least a bachelor's degree to be hired though some employers do not. Employers will often look for a degree in software development though will hire people with degrees in computer science and computer programming. Employers will also look at what education a applicant went to determine the quality of an applicant's education.

Work values

Quality work
All software developers should put out quality work
Personal Development
A software developer should not be constrained to one or a couple languages but should constanly work on learning and understanding more languages
Creativity
A software developer should be able to think out of the box and find solutions to complex problems
Engagement
A software developer should be engaged with their work and always stay on track and on task
Teamwork
A software developer will often work with others on a team and should know how to work well with others and within a team
Work ethic
A software developer should have good work ethic such as finishing projects on time and turning in quality work
