Explore Rome

Rome the city on the Tiber. It has stood for over 2500 years and is among the most influential and important cities in the world. Since the times of the Romans it has been an influential city both politically,culturally, and artistically. This is most appearent in the saying of "Omnes Viae Romam Ducunt" translated to all roads lead to rome to symbalize the power and influence of the city on the western world. After the fall of the Romans and during the the mediveal and renisance ages Rome was a capital of religion for catholics for Rome was home to the Pope and as such Rome was the center of the Catholic world. In to the modern ages with unification of Italy Rome became the capital of Italy and once again the center of the Italian world. Rome has come along way since the citys founding by the Romans and its place in both the western world and history. Rome is a vibrant city with many people and even more things to do and experince, from the ancient ruins of the collesum, Flavian ampitheater,Aurelian walls, and trajans arch. Or if you want to experince the Cities wide range of delicacies there are a plethora of restruants and cafes to treat you to the taste of Italy.

The Flag of Rome

The Roman Flag or the flag of the comune of Rome is a bi-color red and yellow flag. It is sometimes seen with the coat of arms of Rome in the middle of the flag. The coat of arms is a red shield with a crown atop it and a cross followed by the letter SPQR arrayed diagnoly across it. This is in reference to the Roman Republics abbreviation of "Senatus Populusque Romanus", meaning the Senate and the people.
